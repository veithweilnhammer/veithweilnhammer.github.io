I am a psychiatrist, psychotherapist and researcher currently affiliated with Charité - Universitätsmedizin Berlin and the Berlin Institute of Health, where I specialize in studying the neural basis of conscious perception and its alteration in psychotic disorders. My research methodology encompasses a multidisciplinary approach that includes behavioral experiments, computational modeling, functional magnetic resonance imaging, and transcranial magnetic stimulation.

I am excited to announce that I will be joining the laboratory of Doris Tsao at UC Berkeley as a fellow of the Leopoldina Academy of Sciences in August 2023. Should you wish to contact me, I can be reached at my email address, veith.weilnhammer[at]gmail.com.

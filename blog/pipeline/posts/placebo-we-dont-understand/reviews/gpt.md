## Top 3 priorities

1. **Stop equating the whole depression placebo arm with expectation.** Quote: "a large, reproducible share of psychiatric recovery is carried by the expectation-driven response" and closing "accounts for much of the recovery we attribute elsewhere." Fix: say the placebo arm contains natural recovery, regression, assessment effects, contact, and expectation; only part is licensed by the opioid/dopamine/conditioning evidence.
2. **Fix the AI section's overreach and control-group error.** Quote: "talking that carries expectation is exactly what moved opioids and dopamine" and "Most published trials...including the small Woebot study...compare the app against a waitlist or no support." Fix: replace with a narrower claim that chatbot benefit may include expectation/context effects; say Woebot used an information-only control, and more generally many app trials use weak or non-specific controls.
3. **Remove style-guide hard-ban constructions in the opening/closing and AI pivot.** The draft is mostly clear, but several "not X / it is Y" and punchy announcement lines read unlike the house style.

## Style-guide violations

- "Some of what happens in a placebo arm is not a placebo effect at all." Hard-ban negation-definition. Fix: "The placebo arm also contains natural improvement, regression to the mean, and repeated-measurement effects."
- "The placebo reduced their pain." Too punchy as a one-sentence beat. Fix by folding into the next sentence: "In placebo responders, later naloxone blocked or reduced that analgesia."
- "Imaging studies made the mechanism harder to dismiss." Punchy setup sentence. Fix: "Neuroimaging then linked placebo analgesia to changes in pain-processing and expectation-related brain regions."
- "The size of these responses behaves like a dose." Punchy/abstract setup, and the paragraph then piles three examples. Fix: "Verbal suggestion, prior drug experience, and visible treatment delivery can increase placebo analgesia; cite Benedetti/open-hidden data or cut the hidden-pump clause."
- "Parkinson's disease shows the same pattern in a different neurotransmitter system." Announcement sentence. Fix: lead with the concrete study: "In Parkinson's disease, PET studies found dopamine release after patients were told they had received medication."
- "Part of what makes the response biological rather than a matter of conscious hope is that it can be learned." Antithesis. Fix: "Conditioning shows that placebo responses can run without conscious hope."
- "not because the drug worked better in them." Negation contrast. Fix: "because drug responsiveness changed little while placebo responsiveness fell in the most severe group."
- "This cuts against easy hype and easy dismissal together. It rules out... It equally rules out..." Punchy antithesis/parallelism. Fix: replace the three sentences with one plain comparison-control point.
- "Studying placebo as a mechanism should raise the standard of evidence, not lower it." Hard-ban antithesis. Fix: "Studying placebo as a mechanism requires stronger controls, not weaker ones."
- "the placebo arm of a psychiatric trial is not empty. It contains..." Direct hard-ban "not X / it is Y" closing. Fix: "The placebo arm of a psychiatric trial contains expectation, contact, measurement, and natural recovery, and those components deserve study rather than simple dismissal."
- "attention, a plausible account of what is wrong, a structured ritual, and the expectation..." Piled abstract list. Fix: keep two or three load-bearing ingredients, or split them into concrete claims.

excerpt: 'Model-based fMRI shows a distinct distribution in the neural correlates of hierarchically structured sensory predictions in healthy participants. High-level predictions correlate with activity in supra-modal areas such as orbitofrontal cortex and hippocampus, while low-level predictions are represented by BOLD-activity in primary visual cortex.'

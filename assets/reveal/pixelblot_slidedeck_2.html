The Pixelblot

A Portable Trust Token for the Internet


Veith Weilnhammer | Co-Founder & CEO @pixelblot.ai

Bots are undermining trust in all aspects of digital life.

The Problem

  • Fake users, bots, and synthetic content are undermining digital trust.
  • CAPTCHAs are intrusive and not real-time.
  • Existing identity tools often require personal data and onboarding friction.

Our Solution: Pixelblot

  • An anonymous, real-time, shareable token of humanness.
  • Simple, fast, privacy-preserving.
  • Embed anywhere. Use anytime trust is needed.

How It Works

  • Built on behavioral biometrics + machine learning.
  • Real-time analysis of human interaction patterns.
  • No cursor tracking. No personal data.

Use Cases

  • Gaming Lobbies – Fair play with verified humans.
  • Social Media – Bot-resistant interactions.
  • Dating Apps – Human-only profiles.
  • Online Forums – Identity-free verification for trusted discussions.

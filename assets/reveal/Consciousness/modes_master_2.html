Bimodal Inference

Three Questions



  • Is bimodal inference a perceptual phenomenon?

  • Is bimodal inference related to psychosis proneness?

  • What are the neural correlates of bimodal inference?

Human Behavior

  • Behavioral experiment in 150 healthy human participants
  • Serial detection of gabors between -10° and 10°
  • Random and predictable sequences

Interim Summary

  • Bimodal inference modulates perceptual templates
  • Internal mode speeds up processing, in particular when the expreiment is predictable
  • Hallucination proneness is linked to internal mode processing

Mouse Behavior

  • Behavior and Neuropixel electrophysiology in 550 mice
  • Serial detection of gabors at varying contrast
  • Random and predictable sequences
  • Feedback

Interim Summary

  • External mode increases gamma power
  • Mode is represented in visual cortex, hippocampus, entorhinal cortex, and orbitofrontal cortex
  • Internal mode increases bias in drift diffusion

Open questions

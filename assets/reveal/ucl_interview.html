Key results
  • Scz and NMDAR antagonism are associated with a bias toward the external mode

  • Internal representations determine the content of false alarms, which are more frequent during the internal mode

The function of external and internal modes

Constructing stable internal representations from an ongoing stream of noisy data

  • External mode = Exploration: Dissociation from prior knowledge boosts learning and adaption.
  • Internal mode = Exploitation: Maximizes efficiency and tests existing internal representations.

 Preliminary Data
Neural mechanisms

External and internal modes are associated with

  • Global brain state: Larger pupil size reflects exploratory behavior in external modes.
  • Drift diffusion: Stronger of bias + drift by knowledge during internal modes
  • Online replay: Stronger online replace during the internal mode.
  • Offline replay: Offline replay predicts the balance between external and internal mode.
